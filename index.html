Breaking Bad

About:
"Breaking Bad" is a critically acclaimed television series created by Vince Gilligan. The show originally aired on AMC from 2008 to 2013 and is widely considered to be one of the best television dramas of all time. "Breaking Bad" is known for its complex characters, tight storytelling, and cinematography. The series has won numerous awards and has been praised for its writing, acting, and direction.

Info:
The series follows Walter White, a high school chemistry teacher, who is struggling to make ends meet for his family. After being diagnosed with terminal cancer, White turns to a life of crime, producing and selling meth with the help of former student, Jesse Pinkman, in order to secure his family's financial future. Over the course of the show's five seasons, White transforms from a meek and mild-mannered teacher into a ruthless drug lord, leading to a downward spiral that has disastrous consequences for those around him.
